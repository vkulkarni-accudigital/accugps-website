What is the difference between the All-In-One camera and the MDVR system?

The All-In-One camera is a single device that captures forward-facing road view and driver-facing cabin view simultaneously. It is the fastest install and works for most fleets. The MDVR system supports up to 4 cameras for vehicles that need side, rear, or cargo views in addition to forward and driver coverage.

How does AI-powered driver behavior detection work?

The AccuGPS in-cabin camera uses computer vision to analyze the driver's face and posture in real time. It detects phone use, drowsiness, distraction, and seatbelt compliance. When it spots a safety event, it logs a video clip, sends an alert to the fleet manager, and adds the event to the driver's safety score in the AccuGPS platform.

Does the fleet camera include GPS tracking?

Yes. AccuGPS fleet cameras integrate with the AccuGPS GPS tracking platform. Every recorded event is tagged with the exact location, speed, and time. Fleet managers in Syracuse and Jamesville can replay any incident on the map, see the associated video clip, and review the driver's full trip timeline alongside the camera footage.

Where is video stored and how long is it kept?

AccuGPS fleet cameras store footage locally on an SD card and upload event-triggered clips to cloud storage automatically over 4G LTE. Cloud footage is accessible from the AccuGPS platform on any device. Continuous recording is stored locally and can be pulled on demand. Retention periods vary by plan.
