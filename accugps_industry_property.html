Can I see which technician is closest to a new job request?
Yes. The AccuGPS platform shows all vehicles on a live map at i.accugps.com/map-view. Dispatch can see every technician's current location, current job status, and estimated travel time to any new address in real time. No more calling around to find out who is available.
What happens if a customer says the technician damaged their property?
The forward-facing camera records the driveway approach and departure at every stop. In most dispute cases, the footage shows the condition of the property before and after the visit. Most disputes are resolved immediately when the customer sees the timestamp and footage. Claims that go to insurance are also resolved faster with video evidence.
Does the OBD tracker work in a standard service van?
Yes. Any vehicle with a 2001-or-newer OBD II port can use the plug-in tracker. Most HVAC, plumbing, and landscaping vans and pickups built after 2001 have the port. It takes 2 minutes to install and is live immediately. Older vehicles without an OBD port can use the hardwired 4G LTE tracker.
Can I track after-hours vehicle use for seasonal employees?
Yes. You can set working hours alerts so you receive a notification any time a vehicle moves outside the hours you define. Trip replay stores the full route history for every vehicle so you can review any trip at any time. Seasonal crews and temporary employees are covered the same as full-time staff.
Is there a monthly fee after the first year?
Yes. Service renews at $12.99 per device per month, or $129 per device per year. The first year is included in the $129 purchase price. There are no contracts and no cancellation fees.
