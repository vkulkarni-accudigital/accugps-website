Can AccuGPS generate trip logs for compliance and billing purposes?
Yes. Every trip is automatically logged with pickup time, route, mileage, and drop-off time. Trip replay shows the exact path driven. Reports can be exported for Medicaid billing, state compliance audits, and internal reviews without any manual data entry.
Does the in-cabin camera record passengers?
The AccuGPS All-In-One camera includes a driver-facing lens that captures the cab area. For dedicated passenger compartment coverage in larger vehicles, an auxiliary in-cabin camera can be added to the MDVR system. All footage is stored in the cloud and accessible from the platform.
How does GPS tracking help with on-time performance?
Dispatchers see every vehicle's real-time location on one map. When a vehicle is running behind, the dispatcher can reroute it, notify the facility, or reassign the run in seconds. Drive Score also identifies drivers whose habits cause delays through excessive idling or inefficient routing.
What happens if a patient claims they were injured during transport?
Cloud-stored video from the trip is available immediately from the AccuGPS platform. Footage shows vehicle movement, driver behavior, and in-cab conditions for the full duration of the run. This evidence is used to confirm or deny claims without relying on driver recollection alone.
Is there a monthly fee after the first year?
Yes. Service renews at $12.99 per device per month, or $129 per device per year. The first year is included in the $129 purchase price. There are no contracts and no cancellation fees.
