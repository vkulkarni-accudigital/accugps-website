Does AccuGPS automatically generate IFTA mileage reports?
Yes. AccuGPS records mileage by state on every trip automatically. At the end of each quarter, IFTA mileage data is available in the platform without any manual entry or spreadsheet work. This reduces audit risk and eliminates the hours typically spent compiling fuel tax records.
How do fleet cameras help with accident liability for trucking companies?
Forward-facing footage documents what happened before, during, and after a collision. When a third party files a claim against your driver, video evidence from the AccuGPS platform is available immediately. Cases that would take months to settle are often resolved quickly once footage is reviewed.
Can I track both the tractor and the trailer separately?
Yes. The OBD II or 4G LTE hardwired tracker goes on the tractor. The Magnetic Asset Tracker attaches to the trailer with no wiring. Both appear on the same platform map. You can see if a trailer has been dropped at a facility or moved without the truck.
Does the MDVR camera system support auxiliary cameras for the trailer?
Yes. The AccuGPS MDVR system supports up to 4 cameras. Operators typically run forward-facing, driver-facing, and one or two auxiliary cameras covering the trailer door or cargo area. All feeds are recorded and accessible from the cloud platform.
Is there a monthly fee after the first year?
Yes. Service renews at $12.99 per device per month, or $129 per device per year. The first year is included in the $129 purchase price. No contracts and no cancellation fees.
